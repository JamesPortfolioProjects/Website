Technical Project Details

Water Quality Tester

Built using Arduino with complex design challenges, from maintaining water pressure to fitting sensors, batteries, and user-friendly LCD displays. Constraints included budget, space, and time, and the final solution involved simplifying output for end-users using recommended safety thresholds from the National Health Services. Collaborated with a team of three using SolidWorks and manual mockups.

AI Eye Tracker

This Python-based project faced threading challenges, solved through queue systems and predictive velocity smoothing. It included GPIO input requirements solved with a custom-built foot pedal. We demonstrated the project on a large display at the showcase; it was received enthusiastically by visitors and children. Team of three developers, requiring extensive creative problem solving.

Magic Mirror

Built solo as a personal project. Used a Raspberry Pi, a custom Flutter app for smartphone control, and an acrylic two-way mirror with custom server hosting for secure communication. Overcame cost and complexity constraints while learning two new coding languages and implementing IP masking on GitHub-hosted servers. Future goals include touch screen and voice controls.
